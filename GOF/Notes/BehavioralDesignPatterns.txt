Behavioral Design Pattern (Communication Between Objects)
	1. Chain of Responsibility(), 
	2. Command(constructor injection),  
	3. Observer(), 
	4. Iterator(add objects to list and iterate), 
	5. State(card inserted or not inserted), 
	6. Template Method(abstract template inherited classes), 
	7. Visitor(), 
	8. Strategy(constructor injection and so on), 
	9. Mediator(send message), 
	10. Memento(create object, add to list, get by index), 
	11. Interpreter(MM-DD-YYYY, DD-MM-YYYY, YYYY-MM-DD, and DD-YYYY)
